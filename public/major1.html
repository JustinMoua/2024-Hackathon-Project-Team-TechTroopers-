Xenobiology and Alien Studies

Are you looking for a career in the field of Xenobiology and Alien Studies? If so, you're in the right place!

At Oklahoma Space University, we provide our students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in

this exciting field. Our Xenobiology and Alien Studies majors are dedicated to exploring the mysteries of

extraterrestrial life and the fascinating world of alien civilizations.
We have a 4-year degree plan that students are able to follow and provide ample data regarding

student statistics for researchers, prospective students, and parents alike to gain a better

understanding of where we academically stand.

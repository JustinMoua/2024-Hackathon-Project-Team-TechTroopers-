Elemental Alchemy and Transmutation

Elemental Alchemy and Transmutation is a branch of science that deals with the study of elements not only founded

in Earth, let alone our Universes, but others as well. Students are able to gain an understanding of how they interact

with one another to prepare them for their future careers whether it be working on Earth or other Universes.
We have a 4-year degree plan that students are able to follow and provide ample data regarding student

for researchers, prospective students, and parents alike to gain a better understanding of where we academically stand.
